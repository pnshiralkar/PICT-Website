Gymnasium

PICT has well equipped Gym facility for students and staff with specially appointed trainer.

The timings for gym is 4 pm to 8 pm.

i. The students make use of the facility on day-to-day basis.

ii. The Gym is made available for students and staff.

iii. The facility is used for conducting Yoga / Meditation.

Facilities

There are many options to travel in the campus.

i. Outdoor and indoor sports facilities are available for volleyball, basketball, chess, carrom, table-tennis, etc.

ii. Hired ground/facilities are available for cricket, shuttle badminton, etc.

iii. Students are motivated/encouraged to take part in various tournaments at College/District/ University/ State/National level.

Management

i. Physical Instructor is exclusively appointed to look after the gym.

ii. Physical Instructor and members report to the sports-in-charge who in turn report to

the Head of the Institution, regularly.

Internet Facility

PICT provides a wide range of Info-Tech facilities and services with a state-of-the-art Networking environment to support all stake holders for betterment of Learning, Research, Teaching, and Administration process. Entire Intranet is facilitated with Structured cabling and all departments are connected with Fiber backbone. It consists of more than 17 km of structured cabling and 1Gbps fiber backbone connectivity. More than 55 Manageable Fast Ethernet switches connects around 1500+ workstation/nodes in campus ensuring local connectivity of each machine at 100 Mbps/1Gbps speed. PICT has Central-Administration-Server-Room with all time UPS backup for undisturbed campus connectivity to Internet.

PICT provides following Internet/Intranet facilities in campus

• Gateway /Firewall server for internet link-Fiber link (155.52 Mbps)
• Gateway /Firewall server for internet link-RF link (50 Mbps)
• DHCP server for WIFI facility
• Antivirus server (Kaspersky Antivirus 10.3.2)
• IEEE access facility through gateway.
• Dedicated Server for online examination conduction of SPPU Exams.
• Network Attached Storage of 12TB Accessible in Intranet.
• Group based and Dedicated high speed Internet Facility for T&P activities.
• Provision of VOICE/VIDEO service as on demand.
• All time Campus Wi-Fi monitoring.
• Providing Wi-Fi registration with MAC & OTP based authentication.
