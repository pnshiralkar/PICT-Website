Art Circle

PICT Art Circle is the Cultural group of the college that officially represents the college at various levels. It also conducts and performs various shows on campus for the students. Students from all classes and departments are welcome here. The PICT Art Circle is a place where you start at nothing, but in time can become a master in numerous things like music, painting, acting, etc.

This cultural group provides students with an enriching experience and exposure to various prestigious platforms. The PICT Art Circle has a glorious past of performing excellently in the Purushottam Karandak and the Firodiya Karandak competitions. It has also left a significant mark in the Daajikaka Gaadgil Karandak and SPPU One Act Play competitions

Pictoreal

PICTOREAL, as you split the word, PICT-O-Real, is not just a collection of articles, but cherished
memories of the BEs, expression of enthusiasm of TEs for the final year, self-oriented department
obsessed SEs and the beginning for all the FEs. PICTOREAL, our college magazine, reflects the manifold
shades of learning and co-curricular activities including the creative and innovative ideas of young
talents. PICTOREAL is the platform to showcase the inherent qualities possessed by each one. It is a
tradition of PICT, awaited by every PICTian !

Events:

1. Donation
The measure of life is not its duration, but its donation.
Every year, PICTOREAL team conducts donation camps and donates money, groceries or other items as
required and requested by the NGOs, Orphanages or Ashrams. PICTOREAL also tries to make a visit to
these places. This year the entire college contributed and donated money and computers to Sawalee Ashram for the orphans based in Ahmednagar.
A Blood donation camp is also organized by the team which is contributed to by both staff and students.

2. Interviews:
PICTOREAL team interviews eminent personalities around the country. Last year we interviewed Mr. Achyut Godbole, the Quora star Mr. Balaji Vishwanathan, the famous writer Ashwin Sanghi and the CEO of Mithi softwares Mr. Tarun Malviya.

3. Pics-O-Reel:

It is an exhibition and competition, a platform for our students and staff members to showcase their talent
in sketching, painting and photography. The visitors cast their votes for their favorites and winners are
announced.

4.PICT-O-Social
The social services arm of PICTOREAL, carries out career awareness activities in the local schools which
lack such facilities.PICT-O-Social was established in 2014. It has carried out volunteering work with many organizations like Matoshree(Orphanage and home for the aged) and Asha Foundation(for girls). The work includes making educational videos for the hearing impaired students and
making writers available for visually impaired students in competitive exams amongst many other things.
